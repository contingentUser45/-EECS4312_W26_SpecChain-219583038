# Application: Calm - Pipeline Reflection

-   **Biggest and most crucial difference between pipelines:**  
- With the automatic pipeline, we saw the least amount of ambiguity at and the highest traceability, when using exactly the same 1678 reviews dataset. Auto captured high level user types with the strongest grounding in specific review segments. In contrast, the manual pipeline produced more focused and nuanced personas that were directly tied to particular review groups, resulting in better relevance to the actual context of the reviews.
-   **Most useful pipeline:**  
- The Automatic pipeline was the most useful overall as it effectively with specially created prompts. This resulted in outputs that were both structured and meaningfully linked to personas, making them more practical for downstream tasks.
-   **Most surprising finding:**  
- With the high testability rating and best review coverage out of all pipelines, the automated pipeline shows that with the right prompt creations and commands, it can gain the best results
-   **Observed weakness in the automated pipeline:**  
- Even though it masters all the fields relevant to the system, the review coverage may not fully reach as effectively as manual input.
